# 13 shader-constants-unification -refactor

## Summary

`STYLE_FLAG_*` decoration constants and related shader constants are defined in three
separate places: `shaders/decoration_constants.h` (C++ macros), `shaders/decoration_constants.glsl`
(GLSL `#define`), and hard-coded in `shaders/grid.metal`. Changing a flag requires 3 edits
with no compiler enforcing consistency between them.

Flagged by **[Claude]** (bad thing #2). Agreed implicitly by **[Gemini]** (shading magic
constants bad thing #5).

---

## Root Cause

GLSL and Metal have separate shader languages with no shared include mechanism at the
language level. The current approach is manual copy-paste across the three files.
