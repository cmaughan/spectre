## Summary

Provide a `dpi_override` config field that bypasses OS DPI detection. Useful for users on mixed-DPI setups, remote desktop sessions, or when the OS reports incorrect PPI (common with certain monitors and KVM switches). The `display_ppi_` member already exists in `App`, making the plumbing straightforward.
