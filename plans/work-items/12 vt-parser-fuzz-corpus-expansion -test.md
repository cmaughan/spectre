## Problem / Gap

`vt_parser_fuzz_tests.cpp` exists and runs under libFuzzer, but the seed corpus is small. Real-world terminal emulators (xterm, foot, wezterm, Kitty) maintain fuzz corpora that cover:
- Multi-byte OSC bodies (`OSC 8 ; params ; URI ST`)
- Sub-parameter CSI sequences (`38:2:r:g:b`, `48:5:n`)
- Malformed UTF-8 mid-sequence (byte dropped in the middle of a codepoint)
- DEC private sequences with unusual parameter counts
- APC and PM sequences that should be silently ignored

Importing these entries would surface VT parser edge cases that the small Draxul corpus misses.
