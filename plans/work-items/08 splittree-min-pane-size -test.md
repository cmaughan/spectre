## Problem

`split_tree_tests.cpp` exists but likely does not cover ratio clamp behaviour when the window is resized below the minimum viable pane size. Negative or zero-size ratios that escape the clamp logic cause divide-by-zero or out-of-bounds viewport calculations downstream.
